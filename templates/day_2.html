Today I finished Chapter 13 of the RedHat 9 EX200 Cert Guide book. This chapter was about different ways of how logging is performed at the OS level and how an admin should use the logs for system troubleshooting. I really do not want to discuss theoretical stuff in this blog post. Today, I want to share with you some ideas regarding the approach that I should adopt in order to learn faster the knowledge presented in the book. What I am thinking at the moment is that I should try to structure all the theoretical stuff in some mindmap diagram or something like that, a diagram that contains the most important keywords, filepaths and things like that, linked to a short explanation. Also, maybe I should create another diagram that contains the actions that an administrator should be able to perform after each chapter. Another idea that comes to my mind is to create a project that automates this tasks.

I think that I will start to implement all of the above and come with an update. Wish me luck.

    About this page

    This is the landing page of my personal blog 🙋. To be frank, I am not sure how this blog is going to look like a year from now. I am planning to write down posts regarding things I learn from time to time, some thoughts, reviews of some places I've been to and also insights of the projects that I am working on.

    When I think of this blog and how I want it to look like a year from now, I like to think that it will gather around a community of people that share same interests as mine, people with whom I would like to get in touch and share ideas and thoughts. I am eager to see how the future will look like for this project.
